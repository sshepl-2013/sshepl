About BKT

Balkrishna Industries — India's Off-Highway Tyre Giant

Founded in 1987 in Mumbai, Balkrishna Industries Ltd. (BKT) has grown into a global leader in off-highway tyres. Listed on BSE and NSE, BKT exports to over 130 countries and ranked among the world's top tyre manufacturers.

BKT specializes in high-performance tyres for agricultural, industrial, construction, mining, and commercial vehicle applications. Their all-steel radial construction delivers exceptional cut resistance and longevity on India's toughest terrains.

Through SSP Sales Pvt. Ltd., the SSHEPL Group now brings BKT's full commercial range to fleet operators, contractors, and transporters across Pune, Nashik, and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.
