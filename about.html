About SSHEPL

S.S. Heavy Equipments Pvt. Ltd. (SSHEPL) was established in 2013 to explore and expand the product business in the field of construction, earthmoving, and allied machinery.

With years of combined experience in corporate and government sales, after-sales service, and team management, the directors bring deep industry expertise and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.




Our Journey


2014 – 2018
SSHEPL began its operations as an authorized dealer for Kobelco Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd., handling excavator sales and service across 16 districts in Maharashtra. The company’s network included a head office in Pune and branches in Kolhapur, Solapur, Nanded, and Aurangabad (now Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar). Kobelco operations concluded in July 2018.
2018
In November 2018, SSHEPL became the authorized dealer for CASE New Holland Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd., representing its entire product range in Pune and Satara districts.
2019
On our performance basis, Sangli and Kolhapur districts were added to SSHEPL’s territory in July 2019. We were no. 1 dealer in West Zone for machine sales.
2020
Building on continued excellence, Solapur, Osmanabad (now Dharashiv), Latur, and Nanded districts were also extended to SSHEPL from March 1, 2020 for our excellent performance in the calendar year 2019.
2023
In 2023, SSHEPL started the dealership of ELSA – Concreting Machinery for its Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar branch.
2025
In late 2025, SSHEPL entered into an association with BKT Tyres, beginning its journey in the tyres business segment. From 2026, SSHEPL plans to commence distributorship for BKT commercial tyres in the Pune, Nashik, and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar regions, expanding its presence in the commercial mobility segment.
