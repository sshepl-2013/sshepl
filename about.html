Who We Are

S.S. Heavy Equipments Pvt. Ltd.

Founded in 2013, S.S. Heavy Equipments Pvt. Ltd. (SSHEPL) is one of Maharashtra's most trusted names in construction equipment — authorized dealer for CASE Construction Equipment, backed by a team of 50+ professionals across 7 cities.

We began as an authorized Kobelco dealer covering 16 districts of Maharashtra with 5 offices. Over the years, our consistent performance and customer-first approach earned us expanded territories, national awards, and the trust of hundreds of contractors, infra companies, and government bodies across the state.

In 2025–26, we added a new chapter — launching SSP Sales Pvt. Ltd. as an authorized BKT Tyre Distributor for Pune, Nashik, and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar territories.

Today SSHEPL is not just a dealer — we are a full 3S partner: Sales, Spares, and Service. Our workshops, field teams, and spare parts network ensure that every machine we sell stays running at peak performance throughout its lifecycle.
