Padfoot Drum · Cohesive Soils
CASE 1110EX-PD

The 1110EX-PD is the padfoot drum variant of CASE's award-winning EX Series compactor — built specifically for cohesive soil compaction. While the 1110EX-D handles gravels and stone, the PD variant uses a padfoot drum that kneads and densifies clay and loam through a different compaction mechanism. This makes it the go-to compactor for Maharashtra's Marathwada region where black cotton soil (expansive clay) is dominant on road projects. The same powerful FPT S8000 engine delivers 34,531 kgf applied force — even higher than the smooth drum variant — for maximum compaction output in clay-heavy conditions.
