CASE 1650M

The 1650M is where the M Series begins — and it's a significant step up from the L Series. At 17,123 kg with a 5 m³ blade capacity and 150 HP FPT Tier 4 engine, it's built for heavy earthmoving, large-scale land clearing, and demanding road sub-grade work on Maharashtra's expressway projects. Comes with CASE ProCare — 3-year/3,000-hour full machine warranty plus a 3-year planned maintenance contract.
