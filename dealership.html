Our Dealerships

We are proud to offer a comprehensive range of equipment from two of the industry's most respected names: Case Construction Equipment and ELSA Concreting Machinery.






Case Construction Equipment is an Italian‑American manufacturer and a global leader in construction machinery, with roots dating back to 1842. As a brand of Case New Holland Industrial, Case is known for its strong values and practical innovation, producing a full line of equipment designed for productivity, power, and durability worldwide. They were the first company to introduce the factory‑integrated backhoe loader.

Our Distributorship


Balkrishna Industries Limited (BKT), headquartered in Mumbai, India, is a leading global manufacturer of off-highway tires since 1987, specializing in durable, high-performance tires for agricultural, industrial, construction, mining, and commercial vehicles. SS Heavy Equipment Private Limited proudly announces its new distributorship for BKT commercial tires, covering Pune, Nashik, and Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, focusing on robust solutions for trucks, tippers, and heavy-duty transport in demanding on/off-road conditions. BKT's commercial tire range features all-steel radial constructions with cut/chip resistance, enhanced grip, and longevity for mining, quarries, ports, and logistics—ideal for India's rugged terrains.
