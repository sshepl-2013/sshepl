Long Undercarriage · Enhanced Stability
CASE CX220C LC

The CX220C LC is the long-undercarriage variant of CASE's flagship Indian excavator — adding 190 kg and longer track length compared to the standard CX220C to deliver enhanced stability on soft and uneven ground. At 22,220 kg, the wider track footprint distributes ground pressure more evenly — making it the preferred choice for excavation on waterlogged sites, soft alluvial soils along Maharashtra's river systems, and demolition work where a low center of gravity is critical. Same 157 HP FPT engine and CIHS hydraulics as the CX220C, with the added stability of the LC configuration.
