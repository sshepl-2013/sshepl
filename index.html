Our History

Founded in 2013 in Pune by Mr. Santosh Chougule, Mr. Prasad Diwakar, and Mr. Sachin Deshmukh, S.S. Heavy Equipments Pvt. Ltd. (SSHEPL) was established to explore and expand the dealership business in the field of construction, earthmoving, and allied machinery. With over 20 years of combined experience in corporate and government sales, after-sales service, and team management, the directors bring deep industry expertise and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Journey

2014 – 2018
SSHEPL began its operations as an authorized dealer for Kobelco Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd., handling excavator sales and service across 16 districts in Maharashtra. The company’s network included a head office in Pune and branches in Kolhapur, Solapur, Nanded, and Aurangabad(now Chh. Sambhabhinagar). Kobelco operations concluded in July 2018.

2018
In November 2018, SSHEPL became the authorized dealer for CASE New Holland Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd., representing its entire product range in Pune and Satara districts.

2019
On our performance basis, Sangli and Kolhapur districts were added to SSHEPL’s territory in July 2019. We were no. 1 dealer in West Zone for machine sales.

2020
Building on continued excellence, Solapur, Osmanabad(now Dharashiv), Latur, and Nanded districts were also extended to SSHEPL from March 1, 2020 for our excellent performance in the calendar year 2019.

2023
In 2023, SSHEPL started the dealership of ELSA – Concreting Machinery for its Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar branch.

    We are proud to offer a comprehensive range of equipment from two of the industry's most respected names: Case Construction Equipment and ELSA Concreting Machinery.

    Case Construction Equipment is an Italian-American manufacturer and a global leader in construction machinery, with roots dating back to 1842. As a brand of Case New Holland Industrial, Case is known for its heritage of strong values and practical innovation, producing a full line of construction equipment designed for productivity, power, and durability worldwide. They were the first company to introduce the factory-integrated backhoe loader.


    ELSA Concreting Machinery India Pvt. Ltd. is an emerging Indian MNC specializing in concreting and construction machinery, often built on Indian and European technology collaboration. Their mission is to support the Construction, Concreting, and Infrastructure industry by supplying machinery that meets high standards of quality and efficiency. ELSA is a pioneer in the field, offering a wide base of products for concrete production and handling.
