CASE 845C

The 845C is CASE's next-generation medium grader — upgraded from the proven 845B platform with the torque converter lock-up system that converts hydraulic coupling to a direct rigid connection, allowing faster machine travel between grading passes. This means less time repositioning and more time grading — directly improving production on long road stretches. The 845C adapts effortlessly to different site conditions: from precise sub-grade preparation on urban road projects to rough rural road leveling across Maharashtra's interior districts.
